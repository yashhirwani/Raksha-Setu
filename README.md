# 📖 Overview

Raksha Setu is a tourist safety platform developed as part of **Smart India Hackathon 2025** to address safety challenges faced by travelers in unfamiliar environments.

The platform provides tourists with real-time safety assistance, emergency response tools, location-based monitoring, and incident reporting capabilities while enabling authorities to respond more effectively during emergencies.

By combining technology with public safety infrastructure, Raksha Setu aims to create a secure and reliable travel experience for both domestic and international tourists.

---

# 🎯 Problem Statement

Tourists often face challenges such as:

* Delayed emergency assistance
* Limited awareness of nearby risks
* Difficulty reporting incidents
* Lack of centralized safety support
* Communication barriers during emergencies

Raksha Setu bridges this gap by providing a unified safety ecosystem connecting tourists, authorities, and emergency services.

# ✨ Current Features

### 🚨 One-Tap SOS

Instant emergency assistance with location sharing.

### 📍 Live Location Tracking

Real-time GPS monitoring for enhanced safety.

### 📝 Incident Reporting

Report accidents, suspicious activities, medical emergencies, and safety concerns directly through the platform.

### 🗺️ Interactive Safety Map

Visualize incident locations and nearby safety zones.

### 🌐 Multilingual Support

Designed to assist both domestic and international travelers.

### 🔔 Safety Alerts

Receive important travel safety notifications and updates.

### 👮 Authority Dashboard

Monitor incidents, view reports, and coordinate emergency responses efficiently.
